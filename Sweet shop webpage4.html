About Chocolate Foundation

Is a device for serving chocolate fondue. These examples resemble a stepped cone, stand between 2 and 4 fett tall with a cown at the top and stacked tiers over a basin at the bottom. The basin is heated keep the chocolate in a liquid while the chocolate is melting inside a device so it can be pulled into the centre cylinder then vetrically transported to the top of the foundation by an screw. Chocolate foundation creating the waterfall made of melted chocolate in which food items like strawberries and marshmallows can be dipped.
Types of Chocolate Foundations can be catergorized as commercial-use and personal-use. Commerical chocolate fountains can be catergorized as Auger system and Pump System. Commerical chocolate fountains are in different sizes from between 20 and 50 inches tall; are designed for use in professional environments such as catering

Home chocolate fountains range in size up to about 19" tall. They are primarily made of plastic and may have some stainless steel components (although all-stainless models do exist) and are usually dishwasher safe. Home chocolate fountains normally hold 6 or fewer pounds of chocolate.
